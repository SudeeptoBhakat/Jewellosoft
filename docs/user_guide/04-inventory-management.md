# 4. Inventory Management

Keeping precise track of your jewelry stock is critical. JewelloSoft's Inventory Management system allows you to organize items by category, track weights, and monitor HUID details securely.

## Accessing Inventory

Navigate to **Inventory** via the left sidebar. Here, you will see a master list of all currently stocked items, their categories, and their total weight.

## Adding a New Item to Stock

When new stock arrives from your artisans or wholesalers, it must be entered into the system.

1. Click the **Add New Item** button at the top right of the Inventory screen.
2. **Item Name**: Give the item a clear, descriptive name (e.g., "22K Mens Gold Chain").
3. **Category**: Select the appropriate category (e.g., Chains, Rings, Bangles). If the category does not exist, you can create a new one directly from the dropdown.
4. **HUID**: Input the 6-digit Hallmark Unique Identification number if applicable. This is crucial for compliance.
5. **Gross Weight**: Enter the exact total weight of the item in grams.
6. **Net Weight**: If the item contains stones or enamel, enter the pure metal weight here.
7. **Purity/Karat**: Select the metal purity (e.g., 22K, 24K, 18K).
8. **Quantity**: For unique items, this is usually 1. For bulk, identical small items (like silver toe rings), you can enter a higher quantity.
9. Click **Save Item**. The item is now available to be instantly searched and added during the billing process.

## Managing Stock and Categories

### Categories
Organizing your stock into categories allows for easier reporting and searching. Use the **Categories** tab within the Inventory section to add, edit, or delete groupings like "Earrings," "Necklaces," or "Coins."

### Low Stock Alerts
JewelloSoft monitors your inventory levels. If you sell the last remaining unit of an item, or if bulk stock drops below your predefined threshold, the system will flag the item.
You can view a summary of all low-stock items on the main Dashboard to help you plan your next purchasing order.

## Auditing and Editing

If you discover a discrepancy during a physical stock audit, or if an item's weight needs to be corrected:
1. Locate the item in the Inventory list.
2. Click the **Edit** (pencil) icon next to the item.
3. Update the necessary fields (e.g., correcting the Gross Weight).
4. Save the changes. 

*Note: Changes made to inventory items do not affect previously finalized bills.*
