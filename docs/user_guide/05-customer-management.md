# 5. Customer Management

Building and maintaining long-term relationships with your clients is the foundation of a successful jewelry business. JewelloSoft's Customer Management section acts as your digital Rolodex, storing contact details and complete purchase histories.

## The Customer Directory

Navigate to **Customers** from the left sidebar. This screen provides a searchable directory of everyone who has shopped with you. 

You can use the search bar at the top to quickly find a customer by:
- Name
- Mobile Number

## Adding a New Customer

While you can add customers quickly during the billing process, you can also add them manually directly into the directory.

1. Click **Add Customer** in the top right corner.
2. Fill out the customer profile:
   - **Full Name**: The customer's legal name.
   - **Mobile Number**: Primary contact number (crucial for sending digital receipts or promotional updates).
   - **Address**: The customer's residential or shipping address.
   - **Email**: (Optional) For sending digital invoices.
   - **PAN/Aadhar**: (Optional) Required for transactions exceeding certain government-mandated thresholds.
3. Click **Save Profile**.

## Viewing Customer History

Understanding a customer's purchasing habits helps provide personalized service.

1. Click on any customer's name in the directory to open their detailed profile.
2. **Profile Details**: View and edit their contact information.
3. **Purchase History**: A chronological list of every invoice and estimate generated for this customer.
   - You can see the date of purchase, total bill amount, and whether they exchanged old metal.
   - Click on any specific invoice in the history list to instantly open, review, or reprint the PDF receipt for that transaction.

## Managing Balances

If your shop allows trusted customers to purchase on credit, or if they have paid an advance for a custom order, their profile will display their current outstanding balance or advance credit. This balance is automatically updated whenever a new bill involving partial payment or advance deductions is finalized.
