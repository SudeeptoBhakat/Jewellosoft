# 6. Settings & Preferences

The **Settings** section allows you to customize JewelloSoft to fit your shop's specific legal requirements and aesthetic preferences. It is important to configure these settings accurately, as they directly impact how your invoices look and the rates applied to your stock.

## Shop Profile Configuration

Your shop profile contains the legal and contact information printed on every bill.

1. Navigate to **Settings** from the left sidebar and select the **Shop Profile** tab.
2. Ensure the following details are accurate:
   - **Shop Name**: The registered name of your business.
   - **Address**: The physical location of your store.
   - **Contact Information**: Your shop's mobile or landline number.
   - **GSTIN & PAN**: Your government-issued tax identification numbers. If you do not have a GST number, leave this field blank, and the software will automatically hide tax-specific sections on your printed bills.

## Uploading a Watermark Logo

To give your printed invoices a premium and official look, you can upload your shop's logo to be used as a background watermark.

1. In the **Shop Profile** tab, look for the **Watermark / Logo** section.
2. Click the upload button to select an image file from your computer (a transparent PNG or high-quality JPG is recommended).
3. The software will automatically optimize the image opacity so it doesn't interfere with the readable text on the invoice.

## Rate and Making Charge Defaults

As discussed in the [Daily Operations](./02-daily-operations.md) guide, the **Rates** tab is where you manage:
- Today's Gold and Silver market rates.
- Default Making Charges (Flat rate or Percentage based).

It is highly recommended that a manager or owner sets these values at the start of each business day.

## Themes and Appearance

JewelloSoft is built with a dynamic design system to ensure long hours of use remain comfortable.

1. Navigate to the **Appearance** tab within Settings.
2. You can switch between different interface themes:
   - **Noir Gold**: The default, high-contrast dark theme designed to reduce eye strain and look premium.
   - **Classic Light**: A traditional bright interface.
3. Your selected theme will be saved locally and applied automatically every time you launch the software.

## Data Synchronization

JewelloSoft is designed as an "Offline-First" application. This means you do not need an active internet connection to create bills, manage inventory, or serve customers.

However, when an internet connection is detected, the software will quietly sync your new transactions and updated customer profiles to the secure cloud. 
- You can view the **Sync Status** indicator in the bottom left corner of your screen. 
- A green indicator means your data is fully backed up.
- A yellow indicator means the software is waiting for an internet connection to perform its backup.
