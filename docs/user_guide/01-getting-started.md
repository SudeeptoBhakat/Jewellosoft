# 1. Getting Started

Welcome to **JewelloSoft**! This guide will walk you through launching the software for the very first time, registering your shop, and familiarizing yourself with the core interface.

## First Launch & Shop Registration

When you launch JewelloSoft for the very first time, the software requires a one-time registration process to configure your local database and secure your shop's data.

1. **Open JewelloSoft**: Double-click the application icon on your desktop.
2. **Registration Screen**: You will be presented with a setup form. Fill in the required details:
   - **Shop Name**: The official name of your jewelry shop (this will appear on all your invoices).
   - **Owner Name**: Your full name.
   - **Mobile Number**: Your primary business contact number.
   - **Password**: Create a strong password. You will use this to securely log in each day.
3. **Activation**: Click the **Register** button. The software will securely establish your shop's profile and prepare the offline database for immediate use.

> **Important**: Keep your password safe. It restricts access to sensitive financial and inventory data.

## Logging In

For daily use, you will be greeted by the Login Screen:

1. Ensure your registered Mobile Number is selected or entered.
2. Enter your secure Password.
3. Click **Login** to access your dashboard.

## Understanding the Interface

JewelloSoft is designed with a modern, easy-to-read "Noir Gold" interface that reduces eye strain during long business hours.

### The Sidebar (Left Navigation)
The sidebar on the left side of your screen provides quick access to all major sections of the software:
- **Dashboard**: Your daily summary, including total sales and current rates.
- **Billing**: The core section for creating estimates and tax invoices.
- **Inventory**: Where you manage your stock, HUIDs, and categories.
- **Customers**: Your directory of all clients and their purchase histories.
- **Settings**: Shop configuration, rate management, and visual themes.

### The Top Navigation Bar
The top bar is visible on every screen and provides immediate access to essential features:
- **Current Rates**: A quick view of today's Gold and Silver rates.
- **Quick Add**: A plus (`+`) button allowing you to rapidly jump into a new invoice or add a new customer without navigating away from your current screen.
- **Profile Menu**: Located in the top right, this allows you to access quick settings or securely log out at the end of your shift.

Now that you are familiar with the basic setup and navigation, proceed to **[Daily Operations](./02-daily-operations.md)** to learn how to set your daily rates and start your workday.
