# 3. Billing & Invoicing

The Billing section is the heart of JewelloSoft, designed to create accurate, professional, and compliant invoices rapidly while a customer is at your counter.

## Creating a New Invoice

To start a new transaction, navigate to **Billing** from the sidebar and click **New Invoice**.

### 1. Selecting the Customer
At the top of the invoice screen, you can assign the bill to a customer.
- **Walk-in Customer**: Leave the details blank or type a generic name if you do not wish to save their profile.
- **Existing Customer**: Use the search bar to find a customer by their mobile number or name. Their details will auto-fill.
- **New Customer**: Click the `+` icon next to the search bar to quickly add a new customer profile on the spot.

### 2. Adding Items
In the items grid, add the jewelry pieces the customer is purchasing.
- **Description**: Enter the item name (e.g., "Gold Bangle", "Diamond Ring").
- **HUID**: If the item is hallmarked, enter the 6-digit HUID code.
- **Weight**: Enter the precise gross weight in grams (e.g., 12.450).
- **Metal Value**: The system will automatically calculate the base metal value using today's saved rates and the entered weight.
- **Making Charge**: The system will apply the default making charge. You can adjust this manually for this specific item if needed.
- **Total**: The system auto-calculates the final item total.

Click **Add Item** to insert another row. The software guarantees space for up to 5 items on the printed receipt layout.

## Old Metal Adjustments

Customers often bring in old jewelry to exchange. JewelloSoft handles this cleanly, deducting the value before calculating final taxes.

1. Locate the **Old Metal / Exchange** section below the item grid.
2. Enter the description of the old items (e.g., "Old Gold Chain").
3. You can process the exchange by **Weight** (entering the purity and weight to calculate value) or directly by **Value** (entering a flat monetary amount agreed upon).
4. The approved value will be subtracted from the subtotal.

## Taxes, Discounts, and Rounding

Before finalizing the bill, apply any necessary final adjustments:

- **Discount**: Enter a flat currency amount to discount from the total bill.
- **Other Charges**: Add custom fees if applicable (e.g., certification fees).
- **Hallmark Charges**: Add standard hallmarking fees per item.
- **GST Calculation**: JewelloSoft automatically calculates SGST (1.5%) and CGST (1.5%) on the taxable amount.
- **Round Off**: The system automatically rounds the final total to the nearest whole Rupee for cash convenience.

## Payments

Record how the customer paid for their purchase.
- Select the **Payment Mode** (Cash, Card, UPI, Bank Transfer).
- Enter the amount paid. 
- *Note: You can split payments (e.g., ₹10,000 in Cash and ₹25,000 via UPI) by adding multiple payment rows.*

## Printing and Templates

Once the final amount is settled, click **Save & Print**.

JewelloSoft offers professionally designed, A4-sized PDF templates that include your shop's watermark, signature lines, and dynamic layout scaling.

- **Standard Template**: A clean, modern layout highlighting the item breakdown and prominent tax details.
- **Classic Template**: A traditional, highly detailed layout favored by many established jewelers.

The generated PDF will automatically display the exact Net Amount in Words (e.g., "RUPEES FIFTY THOUSAND ONLY") to ensure legal clarity.
